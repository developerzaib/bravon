Refund & Revisions Policy

Last Updated: 30/11/2025

1. Overview

At Bravon Estimates , we work hard to deliver accurate, high-quality construction estimating services tailored to each client's project. Since our work is fully customized and labor-intensive, this policy explains how refunds and revisions are handled.

2. Refund Policy

Due to the substantial time, research, and professional labor involved in preparing construction estimates, all payments are final and non-refundable .

Once work has started — including reviewing drawings, analyzing quantities, or preparing cost breakdowns — refunds cannot be issued under any circumstances.

This applies to all services including:

  • Material Takeoffs
  • Full Cost Estimation
  • Bid Preparation
  • MEP & Civil Estimating
  • Residential & Commercial Estimations
  • Drafting, Design & Pre-Construction Support

3. Revisions Policy

We want you to feel confident before submitting your bid — that’s why we offer unlimited revisions on all estimates.

Revisions include:

  • Adjusting quantities
  • Adding or removing scope items
  • Updating pricing based on market changes
  • Correcting missing or unclear details
  • Recalculating based on updated drawings

Unlimited revisions apply as long as the scope does not completely change. If major new drawings, entirely new project details, or a significantly expanded scope is provided, this may require a new estimate with additional charges.

4. Client Responsibilities

You agree to:

  • Provide accurate, complete project files and details.
  • Communicate changes clearly to avoid misunderstandings.
  • Review estimates thoroughly before bid submission.

5. Turnaround for Revisions

Most revisions are processed within 24–48 hours , depending on project size and complexity. Larger revisions may require additional time.
