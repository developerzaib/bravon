Cookie Policy

Last Updated: 30/11/2025

This Cookie Policy explains how Bravon Estimates uses cookies and similar technologies to recognize you when you visit our website. It details what cookies are, why we use them, and how you can control them.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They help improve your experience, enable functionality, and assist us in understanding how our website is being used.

Why We Use Cookies

We use cookies to:

  • Ensure the website functions properly
  • Improve loading speed and performance
  • Analyze traffic and user behavior
  • Remember your preferences
  • Provide relevant advertising (only with consent)

Types of Cookies We Use

1. Essential Cookies

These are required for the website to operate correctly — you cannot disable them.

2. Analytics & Performance Cookies

Help us understand visitor interactions and improve the website’s performance.

3. Functional Cookies

Store preferences such as language, region, and form data.

4. Advertising Cookies

Used to deliver personalized ads and track marketing effectiveness. These run only if you accept marketing cookies.

Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies may come from third parties such as:

  • Google Analytics
  • Facebook Pixel
  • Microsoft Clarity / Hotjar
  • YouTube or embedded video providers

Managing & Controlling Cookies

You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings:

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy
  • Firefox: Settings → Privacy & Security
  • Edge: Settings → Cookies & site permissions

You can also use browser add-ons to block analytics cookies, such as the Google Analytics Opt-Out Add-on.

Data Protection & Privacy

If cookies collect personal data, we handle it under GDPR, CCPA, or other applicable laws. Non-essential cookies run only after consent.

Children

We do not knowingly collect information from children under 16 through cookies.

Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
