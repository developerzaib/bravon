FAQ’s

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about pricing, delivery time, estimating methods, construction takeoffs, software tools, and how our process works.

What services does Bravon Estimating provide?
We provide complete professional construction estimating & material takeoff services including:
  • Residential & Commercial Estimating
  • Trade-specific Takeoffs (Concrete, Framing, MEP, Finishes & More)
  • Budget & Conceptual Estimating
  • Bid Preparation & Support
  • Value Engineering Suggestions
  • Construction Cost Consulting
How much do your estimating services cost?
Pricing is based on:
  • Scope & trade coverage
  • Project size and complexity
  • Number of sheets/drawings
  • Turnaround time required
Most small projects start at $80–$150.
For an exact quote, email your plans to: info@bravonsvc.com
Do you offer flat-rate or monthly estimating packages?
Yes — many contractors hire us long-term. We offer:
  • Flat-rate project pricing
  • Weekly/monthly unlimited estimating plans
  • Dedicated estimator for your company
This is ideal for busy contractors who need consistent estimating support.
What is your delivery time?
  • Small projects: 24–48 hours
  • Medium projects: 2–4 business days
  • Large commercial: 5–7 business days
Rush service is available for urgent bids.
How accurate are your estimates and takeoffs?
Our estimates are built using:
  • RSMeans Cost Data
  • Historical pricing
  • Local market adjustments
  • High-precision software (PlanSwift, OST, Bluebeam)
We aim for 95–99% accuracy depending on drawing quality.
What software do you use?
Industry-leading tools:
  • PlanSwift
  • Bluebeam Revu
  • On-Screen Takeoff (OST)
  • RSMeans
  • AutoCAD
These ensure accurate takeoffs and clean professional reports.
Do you work with all types of contractors?
Yes — our clients include:
  • General Contractors
  • Subcontractors (all trades)
  • Home Builders
  • Architects & Engineers
  • Developers & Investors
Is my project information confidential?
Absolutely yes. All drawings, pricing, and project details remain **strictly confidential. We never share or reuse client data — ever.
Do you offer revisions if plans change?
Minor revisions are free. Major changes (new drawings, addendums, scope changes) may require a small fee.
How do I get started?
1. Email your drawings to info@bravonsvc.com
2. Receive a free quote
3. Approve & we start
4. Get PDF + Excel files with complete breakdowns
