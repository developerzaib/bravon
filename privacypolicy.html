Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 30/11/2025

At Bravon Estimates , we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and the rights you have regarding your information.

Information We Collect

We collect information in the following ways:

  • Information you provide: Such as your name, email, phone number, and project details submitted through our forms.
  • Automatic data: IP address, browser type, device information, and pages visited.
  • Cookies: Small files stored on your device to enhance user experience and analyze site performance.

How We Use Your Information

Your data is used to:

  • Respond to inquiries and provide requested estimates.
  • Improve website performance and user experience.
  • Support customer service and communication.
  • Analyze traffic and monitor website usage.

Email & Communication

When you contact us or submit a form, we may use your email to communicate updates, send your project estimates, or respond to your messages. We do not sell or share your details with third-party marketers.

Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We use cookies to enhance website functionality and analyze usage. You can disable cookies using your browser settings, but some features may not work properly.

Third-Party Services

We may use trusted third-party tools, such as:

  • Form submission platforms (e.g., FormSubmit)
  • Analytics services

These providers follow strict data protection guidelines, but we are not responsible for their individual policies. Always review their terms when interacting with them.

Data Security

We use reasonable technical and administrative measures to protect your data. However, no digital platform is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee complete security.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request a copy of your stored data.
  • Ask us to update, correct, or delete your information.
  • Withdraw consent when applicable.

Children’s Privacy

Our website is not intended for individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy at any time. Any updates will be posted on this page with a revised “Last Updated” date.
